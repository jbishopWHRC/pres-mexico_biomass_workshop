Segmentation Statistics

For each ALOS scene, we calculate the mean, standard deviation, minimum, and maximum of:

  • ALOS HH
  • ALOS HV
  • ALOS Layover/Shadow
  • SRTM Elevation
  • SRTM Slope
  • Tree Cover
